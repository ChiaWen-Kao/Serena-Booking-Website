About This Event

What You'll Create

In this workshop, you'll have the chance to craft a variety of clay artworks

  1. Sculptures: Learn the art of sculpting and create your own unique clay sculptures. Express your creativity by shaping and molding the clay into three-dimensional forms.
  2. All Materials Included: We provide all the necessary clay, sculpting tools, and equipment. Just bring your creativity!
  3. Take-Home Art: At the end of the workshop, you'll have your own clay artworks to take home and showcase your artistic talent.
